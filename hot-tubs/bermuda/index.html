Bermuda Hot Tub

RP2810L

The Bermuda RP2810L hot tub provides a premium hydrotherapy experience with 52 jets, a 4HP two-speed pump, and user-friendly Gecko K300 controls.

Featuring a sleek acrylic shell, LED lighting, rectangular shape, and spacious seating for more than six people, the Bermuda is perfect for enhancing any outdoor retreat.

The resilient HDPE cabinet provides both visual elegance and robust protection against the elements.

100% Made in the USA.

Specs

  • Size: 93″ x 120″ x 38″
  • Electrical: 240V
  • Pump: 2 x 4HP / 1 & 2 Speed
  • Controls: Gecko K300
  • Jets: 52
  • Lighting: LED
  • Shell color: Silver
  • Cabinet: HDPE
  • Cabinet color: Slate gray
