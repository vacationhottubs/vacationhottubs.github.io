Bermuda Hot Tub

RP177B

The Frisco RP177B Hot Tub provides a premium hydrotherapy experience with 27 jets, a 4HP two-speed pump, and user-friendly Gecko K300 controls.

Featuring a sleek acrylic shell, LED lighting, and spacious seating for six, the Frisco is perfect for enhancing any outdoor retreat.

The resilient HDPE cabinet provides both visual elegance and robust protection against the elements.

100% Made in the USA.

Specs

  • Size: 84″ x 84″ x 36″
  • Electrical: 240V
  • Pump: 1 x 4HP / 2 Speed
  • Controls: Gecko K300
  • Jets: 27
  • Lighting: LED
  • Cabinet: HDPE
  • Shell Color: Sierra
