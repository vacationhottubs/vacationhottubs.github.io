Laguna Hot Tub

RP288B

The Laguna RP288B hot tub offers a spacious and premium hydrotherapy experience with 50 jets, dual 4HP pumps, and intuitive Gecko K300 controls.

Featuring a sleek acrylic shell, LED lighting, and ample seating for up to seven, the Laguna is the perfect addition for creating a luxurious outdoor retreat.

The resilient HDPE cabinet provides both visual elegance and robust protection against the elements.

100% Made in the USA.

Specs

  • Weight: 900 lbs
  • Size: 93″ x 93″ x 36″
  • Electrical: 240V
  • Pump: 2 x 4HP / 1 & 2 Speed
  • Controls: Gecko K300
  • Jets: 50
  • Lighting: LED
  • Shell color: Sierra
  • Cabinet: HDPE
  • Cabinet color: Slate gray
