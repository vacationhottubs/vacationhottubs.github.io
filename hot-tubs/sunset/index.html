Sunset Hot Tub

RP28RB

The Sunset RP28RB hot tub provides a luxurious hydrotherapy experience with 52 powerful jets, dual 4HP pumps, and intuitive Gecko K300 controls.

Featuring an elegant round design, LED lighting, and spacious seating for up to seven, the Sunset is ideal for creating a relaxing backyard retreat.

The resilient HDPE cabinet provides both visual elegance and robust protection against the elements.

100% Made in the USA.

  • Size: Round 92″ x 40″
  • Electrical: 240V
  • Pump: 2 x 4HP / 1 & 2 Speed
  • Controls: Gecko K300
  • Jets: 52
  • Lighting: LED
  • Shell color: Silver
  • Cabinet: HDPE
  • Cabinet color: Slate gray
