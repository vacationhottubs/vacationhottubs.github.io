Buxton Hot Tub

RP166L

The Buxton RP166L hot tub provides a premium hydrotherapy experience with 29 jets, a 4HP two-speed pump, and user-friendly Gecko K300 controls.

Featuring a sleek acrylic shell, and LED lighting, the Buxton is perfect for enhancing your vacation home.

The resilient HDPE cabinet provides both visual elegance and robust protection against the elements.

100% Made in the USA.

  • Pump: 1 x 4HP / 2 Speed
  • Controls: Gecko K300
  • Jets: 29
  • Lighting: LED
  • Shell color: Sierra
  • Cabinet: HDPE
  • Cabinet color: Slate gray
